## 1. Purpose

In SN5 basically all of our ~$5.3k spend went through the lead's personal card, reimbursements were slow, and needs that surfaced late turned into rush-shipping blowouts ($400 of shipping on $120 of ACP rods — PP-08). The team-wide purchasing process actually exists and is fine — composites members just didn't know it. This doc restates it and adds our ordering calendar.

## 2. Scope

All composites purchases and reimbursements; sponsor material requests at outline level.

## 4. Definitions

Approval tiers, reimbursement form (pinned in #purchasing channel description), purchaser (a member who fronts money and gets reimbursed).

## 5. Equipment & Materials

None — process standard.

## 6. Safety

No physical hazards. Financial safety rule: nobody fronts more than they are comfortable waiting ~a month to get back (R1: deposits take up to a month).

## 7. Procedure (outline — names/tiers as of 2025-26; re-verify each season)

### 7.1 Approval

1. **Every purchase gets approval BEFORE buying**, posted in #purchasing: <$50 → subteam lead; $50–250 → division lead; >$250 → President/VP (R1).

### 7.2 Reimbursement

1. **Reimbursement:** photo of the itemized receipt (card statement alone is NOT enough) + card statement (redact other lines, keep name/address/last-4/purchase/date); submit the pinned form within **1 month**; no Venmo/Zelle/cash/gift cards; Amazon must show Shipped/Delivered; categorize under the subteam **using** the material (R1).

### 7.3 Distributed purchasers

1. The lead designates 2–3 members (with the finance lead's knowledge) as regular purchasers so no single card is the team's float (PP-08).

### 7.4 Order calendar (composites)

1. **Order calendar:** September — bulk consumables + first Easy Composites basket (≥6-week lead, customs margin — PP-02); December — spring-semester basket; rolling — min-stock triggers from CS-011 stock walks. A shared "basket" doc collects wants before each EC order (SN5 practice: "add what you would like by TONIGHT" — make it a standing doc instead).

### 7.5 Rush orders

1. Any order with >2× shipping-to-item ratio needs the lead's explicit sign-off with the alternative (wait/borrow/local) stated — make the $400-shipping decision consciously.

### 7.6 Sponsor asks

1. **Sponsor asks:** batch material requests through the lead (Airtech/Sigmatex/Coastal/ACP/Rexco contacts live with PM); never let sponsor stock run to zero before asking — sponsors have lead times too.

### 7.7 Season close

1. All reimbursements submitted by the finance deadline (SN5: July 4 hard cutoff; unpaid-form escalation window to July 14 — dates change yearly, check #purchasing).
